# 04 - Cross-Functional Refinement

## Purpose

Align product intent with engineering, QA, security, operations, support, analytics, and other impacted teams before planning is finalized.

## Step-by-Step Workflow

1. Review stories and acceptance criteria with impacted functions.
2. Surface hidden complexity, missing requirements, and sequencing risk.
3. Identify integration points, support impact, analytics impact, and operational concerns.
4. Record decisions, open questions, and follow-up owners.
